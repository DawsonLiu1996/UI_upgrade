Editorial

A layout where typography is the product

This template is intentionally calm. It makes long content feel premium through spacing, measure, and rhythm.

Good editorial UI isn’t about color. It’s about reading comfort: consistent widths, predictable headings, and whitespace that guides the eye.

“Whitespace is a navigation system.”
Use it to separate ideas, create emphasis, and make dense information feel approachable.
